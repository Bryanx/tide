Limited time sale on Go-Pass tickets!

29 November 2015

Beginning on the 1st of december untill the 30th of january there will be a limited sale on Go-Pass tickets. Everyone buying a ticket between this time period will receive a 25% discount. This sale is meant to encourage people to book tickets in advance, so we can comprehend the amount of people coming.

Like we've seen last year at the Tide festival, it became much busier than we could have ever anticipated, causing some very busy moments on our festival. This made several complaints on our crowd management and its understandable, people weren't to happy about it. So to prevent this from happening again this year, we're introducing a limited time sale for all you early bookers! This way we know in advance how much crowd management will be neccesary in the future. We can't foresee everything ofcourse, so we hop we've got your suppoert on how we're handeling this. Remember, we're a young festival we learn by each step we take.
