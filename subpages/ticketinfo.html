Tickets

Where do I get tickets?

Tickets can be bought digitally on the Tide website and from the Tide Ambassadors spread all over the world. As usual the Tide ticket prices are decided taking social awareness into consideration: Flexible prices to suit the different economic realities of each country. What kind of tickets can i get? How much do they cost? Are there different kinds of tickets? Do children need a ticket? Children under 13 do not need a ticket, but they must be accompanied by parents or legal tutors. Young adults under 18 years old also need to be accompanied by parents or legal tutors. Note: Considering the high temperatures, loud noise and high levels of dust, we recommend parents not to bring children under 3 years of age!

Ticket pricing

We have chosen to have less ticket phases compared with previous editions to allow you more time to acquire a ticket in each phase (there are three phases in 2016 and not four phases as in 2014). We don’t increase the highest price of the tickets (which remains at 180 €). We applied an average between the previous 3 ticket phases in ​​2014 (120€, 140€, 160€) to obtain an average for the current value of the first two stages in 2016 (130€, 150€). Furthermore, tickets for Tide are limited. In spite of the growing popularity and positive perception about Tide, we are focused on keeping a familiar and cozy vibe, with quality and comfort. Tide is not about quantity. There are 100.000 tickets available for Tide 2016. There are a limited number of tickets per phase. If tickets are Sold Out before the date of the end of the phase, the following Phase begins immediately. Never buy a ticket on auction websites such as Ebay, Olx and many others.
